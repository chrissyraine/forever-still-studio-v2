A Kitchen, Five Kids, and a Whole Lot of Butter

Dirty Dozens Sweets and Treats started the way most great things do — with a mom who refused to buy a grocery store cake one more time. Amanda Waters taught herself to bake between school pickups, soccer practices, and the beautiful chaos of raising five kids.

What began as cupcakes for birthday parties turned into a full-blown obsession. Neighbors started asking. Then their neighbors. Then people across Oil City, PA who'd heard about "that mom who makes the cakes." Amanda never went to culinary school. She didn't need to. She had determination, a stand mixer, and the kind of perfectionism that only a mom of five can develop.

Today, every cupcake, every custom cake, and every last crumb that comes out of Dirty Dozens is still made from scratch — with real ingredients, real care, and the same passion that started it all. Because around here, we don't cut corners. We frost them.
