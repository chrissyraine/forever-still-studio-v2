About

Built for Real People,
Not Corporations

Forever Still Studio is a boutique web design studio based in Titusville, PA — built specifically for small businesses that are done being invisible online.

I'm not a big agency. I don't have a sales team or a pitch deck. What I have is a genuine commitment to making your business look as good online as it does in person.

I'm a local — a veteran, a mom, and someone who knows what it means to build something from nothing. I bring that same work ethic to every website I build.

FAQ

Common Questions

"I already have customers — do I really need a website?"

Your current customers found you somehow — word of mouth, a sign, a recommendation. A website means the next person searching Google at 10pm can find you too. It works while you sleep.

"How long does it take?"

Most sites are live within 4–6 weeks. I work around your schedule, not mine.

"How much does it cost?"

Every project is different. I'll talk through what you need and give you a straight number — no surprises. Reach out and let's figure it out together.

"I'm not tech savvy at all."

Good — you don't need to be. That's what I'm here for.
