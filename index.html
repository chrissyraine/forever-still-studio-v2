WE BUILD FOR PEOPLE, NOT CORPORATIONS

Forever Still Studio is a boutique web design studio based in Titusville, PA — built specifically for small businesses that are done being invisible online.

We're not a big agency. We don't have a sales team or a pitch deck. What we have is a genuine commitment to making your business look as good online as it does in person.

Our founder Christina Schroer is a local — a veteran, a mom, and someone who knows what it means to build something from nothing. She brings that same work ethic to every website she builds.

"I started this studio because the businesses I love in this town deserve better than a template and a prayer. You've worked too hard for a website that doesn't work for you."
— Christina Schroer, Founder

Rooted in Community

As a single mom raising two boys in Titusville, I understand the challenges local businesses face. My family is part of this community, and I'm committed to helping your business thrive here too.
